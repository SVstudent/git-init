Maintain Your Health

Calories!
There is a common misunderstanding that calories are unhealthy. But the truth is tha there's a difference between, "good" calories and "bad" calories.

Nutritions!
There are various reasons why nutritions are important. A healthy diet throughout life promotes healthy pregnancy outcomes, supports normal growth, development and ageing, helps to maintain a healthy body weight, and reduces the risk of chronic disease leading to overall health and well-being. People with healthy eating patterns live longer and are at lower risk for serious health problems such as heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and obesity. Nutritions can decide the life and death of a person.

Stress!
There are many different types of stress for various people. Even short-lived, minor stress can have a major impact. For example, you might get a stomachache before you have to give a presentation. And more major acute stress, whether caused by a fight with your spouse or an event like an earthquake or terrorist attack, can have an even bigger impact. Although stress can have all these negative impacts on the host, stress is naturally a part of our lives. No human being has never not been in stress. So in a way, we have to embrace and be able to control our stress.
